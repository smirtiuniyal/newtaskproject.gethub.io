Simpler

Discover a sophisticated yachting experience with the power to thrill. Simpler can run at 30 MPH, our 33 foot tender screams along at 72 MPH, and our Jet Ski quickly excels to 70 MPH. With 2 Sea Bobs, paddle boards and other toys, Simpler provides fun in the sun for all ages. Simpler is distinctively luxurious and spacious with 3 exceptional crew members that will take care of your every need. We look forward to having you aboard!
