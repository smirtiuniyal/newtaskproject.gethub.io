STATEROOMS

Welcoming up to 8 guests, Simpler has 4 luxurious staterooms each with its own en-suite bathroom, desk and closet.

  • Home
  • Staterooms

Master Stateroom

Enjoy a spacious full beam master with a large bed, couch, desk, makeup vanity, TV, stereo, his and her closets, en-suite bathroom with double sinks, and an over-sized shower with multiple shower heads.

VIP Stateroom

The Forward Stateroom is a second master stateroom with large bed, desk, makeup vanity , TV, stereo, closet and en-suite bathroom.

Starboard Stateroom

This stateroom has a large bed, desk, makeup vanity , TV, stereo, closet and en-suite bathroom.

Port Stateroom

This stateroom has a large bed and with a push of a button it can separate into two berths. Complete with TV, stereo, closet and en-suite bathroom.
